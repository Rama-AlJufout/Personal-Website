Exploring Madrid

Madrid offered a vibrant blend of culture and history. I enjoyed wandering through the Prado Museum, strolling in Retiro Park, and savoring delicious tapas in lively plazas.

Relaxing in Mallorca

The beautiful island of Mallorca was a perfect escape with its stunning beaches and charming villages. I spent my days soaking up the sun and exploring the scenic coastline.

Discovering Granada

Granada captivated me with its rich Moorish history and architecture. Visiting the Alhambra Palace was a breathtaking experience full of intricate designs and beautiful gardens.
